Welcome

I believe therapy can be an opportunity to gain a unique perspective on the challenges occurring in our lives. These challenges can feel overwhelming at times. Getting effective counseling can help you discover your path toward personal enrichment and well-being.

I like to provide a warm and friendly atmosphere; one that allows you to express feelings and explore options in a safe and protected environment, without blame or criticism. I can help you and/or your family resolve and identify solutions to the challenges of many issues, including: anxiety, depression, relationship conflicts, stress, low self-esteem, grief/loss, parenting, trauma, and child/adolescent behaviors.

I have over nine years of experience providing therapy and counseling, with additional training in Parent Child Interactive Therapy (PCIT). I am certified in EMDR, an effective way to treat trauma related issues. I would be honored to have the privilege of working with you.

Please call me for an individual, couples or family therapy consultation today.
